✅ Using Loop
🤖
📩 Message automatically sent to Rahul
📩 Message automatically sent to Aman
📩 Message automatically sent to Priya
📩 Message automatically sent to Neha
📩 Message automatically sent to Arjun
Loops automate repetitive work
people = ["Rahul", "Aman", "Priya", "Neha", "Arjun"]

for person in people:
    send_message(person)
